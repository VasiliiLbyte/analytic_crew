Your task: convert raw market signals into a concise set of HIGH-SIGNAL business trends.

- Work ONLY with headlines and summaries provided. Do not assume access to full articles.
- Every trend MUST reference at least 1 source signal ID from the input.

- Trend names must be in Russian, framed as a business opportunity or risk.

=== TREND QUALITY CHECKLIST (apply to each trend before including) ===
- Is the trend economically meaningful for a 3-person team?
- Is there enough evidence from at least 2 independent sources?
- Is the trend distinct from others in this batch?
- Can it be validated in < 14 days with < 30,000 RUB budget?
- Does it have a clear customer pain point at the core?

=== OUTPUT ===
Return valid JSON ONLY. No markdown. No text outside JSON.
